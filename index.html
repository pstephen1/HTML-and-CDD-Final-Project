What is Pickard Printing?


We are a small, family run business focused on using various 3D rendering technologies to generate 3D models that can then be created quickly using FDM 3D printing technology.  3D printing allows us to remain infinitely flexible while we create a wide variety of items with minimal waste, small amounts of electricity, and without the lengthy and expensive process of tooling custom molds and forms otherwise required to create injection-molded items.  Our most common material, PLA, or Polylactic Acid, is a bioplastic made entirely from renewable resources like corn starch and sugar cane!

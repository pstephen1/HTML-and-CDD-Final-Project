How Can We Help?


Printing & Rapid Prototyping

It's in the name! Bring us your 3D model and we can get to work straight away.  We support various formats like STL, FBX, OBJ, 3DS, PMD, PMX, BLEND, among many others.  If the file contains a 3D model and the format is open source, we'll handle all of the conversion and be happy to get it made for you!

3D Modeling

Don't have a file, but have an idea? We can help!  We can generate a 3D model for you in an STL file. Use the Contact Us form to get in touch with our team and get a quote when you provide either a technical drawing of the item, or if you have nothing more than an idea schedule a live chat session with one of our 3D modelers to let them help you bring your idea to life!

More Coming Soon!

As we grow and expand our little operation we will be adding more services to our lineup.  Services such as laser sintering, CNC milling, bulk ordering options, finishing and polishing, and more!  Check back often to see when we add these services, and make sure to take advantage of them!
